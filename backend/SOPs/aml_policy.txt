AML POLICY - SECTION 4.2
All customer transactions above $10,000 must be reported.
Customer identity must be verified before account opening.
Suspicious transactions must be reported within 30 days.

AML POLICY - SECTION 7.1
Data retention for customer records is 5 years.
Employee training on AML must happen annually.
Third party vendors must sign data protection agreements.

DATA PRIVACY POLICY - SECTION 2.1
Customer data must be encrypted at rest.
Data breaches must be reported within 72 hours.
Customer consent must be obtained before data sharing.

LENDING POLICY - SECTION 3.1
Interest rates must be disclosed upfront.
Loan applications must be processed within 10 business days.
Credit checks must follow fair lending guidelines.
